Manifesto

Bitcoin · AI · Independence · Est. 2022

We refuse to accept a world where the rules are rigged.

The fiat system is ungodly corrupt. It is the closest thing humans have ever created to a time machine for the powerful: governments print money out of thin air, debase our currency, spend without consequence, and leave everyday people and businesses holding the bag of inflation — the silent tax that quietly steals your purchasing power and your future.

Fix the money, fix the world.
That is not a slogan. It is a conviction.

We believe open-source technology combined with the hardest money ever created is the path to true human progress. Bitcoin is backed by the laws of thermodynamics and mathematics — not the promises of politicians. AI is a deflationary force that will drive the cost of goods and services down over time, increasing everyone's purchasing power and unlocking real innovation and happier lives.

We want to see a hard fork in society: individuals and businesses reclaiming their sovereignty, cutting out the middlemen, and settling transactions on the best money humanity has ever known.

That is why Lightning Strategies exists.

The Founder's Story

I didn't always see this clearly. As an economics major I thought I understood finance — until I discovered Bitcoin in 2017. That opened my eyes to the deep corruption and Ponzi-like nature of the fiat system.

I lived the rollercoaster: rode the 2017 bull run, bought heavily during the 2020 COVID crash, sold too early at ~$45k, and still turned that into my dream car, engagement ring, house down payment, wedding, and honeymoon. I paid tuition to the market the hard way — including losing Bitcoin by giving away my private keys.

In 2022 I founded this company with a vision to raise $10–20M for a Bitcoin mining operation. We only raised $1.5M, so we pivoted. I kept mining personally and using the entity to bridge into the new system. Today I live fully on the Bitcoin standard — not as theory, but as daily practice.

And I want the same freedom for you.
